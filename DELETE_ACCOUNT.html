SendR Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: 2026-07-09

If you no longer wish to use the SendR app, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data in compliance with Google Play Store policies and POPIA.

How to request account deletion

Option 1: In-App (Recommended)

If you still have the SendR app installed:

  1. Open the SendR app.
  2. Navigate to your Profile / Settings.
  3. Tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

Option 2: Via Email (Web Request)

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team directly:

  1. Send an email to support@mzansicash.com (please update to your actual support email if different).
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Please include the registered phone number associated with your SendR account in the body of the email.

Note: For security purposes, our support team will contact you via SMS or call on your registered phone number to verify your identity before finalizing the deletion.

What happens when your account is deleted?

  • Your SendR account profile will be permanently closed.
  • Your personal profile information will be deleted from active systems.
  • Any linked bank accounts (Paystack subaccounts) will be unlinked.
  • Regulatory Exceptions: As a financial technology platform, MzansiCash (Pty) Ltd is required by South African law (including the Financial Intelligence Centre Act - FICA) to retain certain transaction records and anti-money laundering (AML) compliance data for a minimum period of 5 years. This data will be securely archived and will not be used for marketing or any other operational purpose.

For more information on data retention, please review our Privacy Policy.
