SendR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-07-05

MzansiCash (Pty) Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the SendR mobile application and services.

This Privacy Policy forms part of our Terms of Use and is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, specifically the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

To provide our P2P payment service securely, we collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Your mobile phone number (used as your primary identifier) and display name.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details (processed securely via Paystack to create your subaccount).
  • Contact List: With your explicit permission, we securely hash your device's contact list to help you discover friends who are already on SendR. We never store your raw contact list on our servers.

1.2 Device & Security Information

To protect your account from fraud, we collect:

  • Device Identifiers: UUIDs generated on your device.
  • Device Metadata: OS version, device model, and app version.
  • Network Data: IP addresses.
  • Location Data: We collect approximate location data (rounded to ~1km precision) during login solely to calculate the distance between login attempts. This prevents impossible travel anomalies (e.g., logging in from Johannesburg and Cape Town within 5 minutes).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process transactions, create your Paystack subaccount, and send notifications.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To enforce our Device Binding architecture. We monitor login patterns and enforce step-up authentication if we detect suspicious activity (like SIM swaps or unauthorized devices).
  • Compliance: To comply with FICA regulations regarding transaction monitoring and anti-money laundering (AML).

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share necessary data with Paystack South Africa to process your payments and settle funds.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to the Financial Intelligence Centre (FIC) or law enforcement if required by law (e.g., suspicious activity reports under FICA).
  • Business Transfers: If MzansiCash (Pty) Ltd is involved in a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security to protect your data:

  • Server-Side Architecture: We employ a "Never Trust the Client" model. All sensitive operations and validations happen on our secure Supabase Edge Functions.
  • App Check: We use Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) to ensure that only our unmodified, official app can access our APIs.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

5. Data Retention

  • Financial Records: Under South African law, we are required to retain transactional records for seven (7) years.
  • Security Logs: Device login attempts and location anomaly logs are retained for ninety (90) days for forensic purposes, after which they are automatically deleted.
  • Account Deletion: If you request account deletion, we will soft-delete your profile to prevent immediate reuse and permanently delete your authentication record, retaining only what is legally required.

6. Your Rights (POPIA)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information (subject to our legal retention requirements).
  • Object to the processing of your personal information.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Information Officer at support@mzansicash.co.za.
